Tracking your entire fleet

Access Real-Time Location and Route History of your fleet

Onelap's user-friendly app lets you access your vehicle's live location and travel history on the go. The highly sensitive Onelap chip sends the exact location of your vehicle so you can track every step of the journey and if your drivers are making any unnecessary stoppages, going too fast, too slow or taking any unnecessary detours.

Reduce the Costs

With Extensive Data optimise the costs involved

With access to data such as Total distance covered, maximum speed, minimum speed and Idling time you can plan your next trip in an optimum manner so that your cost overruns are minimised and your performances optimised. This also helps to predict the fuel spend and how it could be effectively reduced.

Monitor your Personnel

Train and Monitor your employees with Personalised Data

With access to extensive data like maximum/minimum speeds of every vehicle, you can monitor each driver separately and predict their driving habits so that any potentially harmful accidents due to driver negligence and related delays could be averted which can contribute to rising costs in this sector, so efficient monitoring could keep it under check and drivers can be trained to improve their performance.

Increased safety and fast tracking in case of any theft
