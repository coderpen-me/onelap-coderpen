Track all the fleet

Track each and every vehicle separately on a single platform

The Municipal Corporation usually owns a large number of vehicles for the various day-to-day activities that it manages. Managing such a huge fleet is not an easy challenge but fitting the vehicles with Onelap allows you to keep a check on every vehicle separately and allows you to manage your resources in an efficient manner.

Optimization of resources

Use detailed and precise reports to improve performance

With data such as Speed,Distance covered, Route History etc. you can estimate the cost of operation that is being carried out and use it effectively to come up with measures to optimize it for your benefit. This can also be used to review performances of the employees and monitor any non-compliance to the guidelines provided to them

Manage your resources correctly

Deploy the correct service at the right place and time

With the precise location of each and every vehicle of your fleet you can make sure that if a complaint or request is received from a particular area, the resource or service provider which is closest to that location responds to that request, so that the service is prompt and unnecessary wastage of resources available to you is reduced.
