Getting Started in 4 Steps

Step 1

You will Need a Hardware

A Hardware is needed to transmit the real time
location of the vehicle to Onelap cloud server

Step 2

You will Need a Sim Card

The hardware needs internet connectivity to
send the data over GSM network

Step 3

Install the hardware

Get the hardware installed with a help of any
local technician near you

Step 4

Activate the hardware

Download Onelap Telematics App from playstore
and signup and call our customer support team
