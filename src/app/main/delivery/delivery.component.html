Trace every Delivery

Check if delivery is made on time

Since the product has to be delivered quickly, any delays would hamper customer satisfaction. This will reduce the possibility of customer reordering from you in the future, so to ensure that there is no unnecessary delay and the product is delivered within the stipulated time limit you have the access to the real time location of each and every delivery person separately. This data helps you to manage defaulters and ensure optimum service to the customer.

Appraise your Employees

Evaluate performance of your personnel

The employees could be judged on the basis of criteria such as delivery time, stoppages made, maximum/minimum speeds, fuel consumption and the route taken and whether it was optimum or not. Since the employees are aware of the presence of a monitoring system the likelihood of any mistake is decreased.

Estimate best routes and minimise cost

Large database helps you to optimize performance

Since you have access to data from multiple vehicles of your fleet you can estimate the total cost incurred on delivery and the data of routes that were taken can be studied to find out the best possible way to reach a particular destination in the shortest time so that the delivery performance is optimised and customer satisfaction is increased.
